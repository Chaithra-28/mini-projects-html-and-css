WHAT
IS CALLED ART?

Art encompasses a wide range of human creativity, including visual, performing, and literary forms, serving to express ideas, emotions, and experiences, and often intended to evoke a meaningful experience.

7 TYPES OF ART

The seven different types of art are Painiting, Sculpture, Literature, Architecture, Theatre, Film, and Music However, back in the day, the seven arts were called the liberal arts, consisting of Grammar, Rhetoric, Logic, Geometry, Arithmetic, Music, and Astronomy.
